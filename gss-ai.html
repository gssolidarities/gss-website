The premise

Most models are trained to be everything to everyone — and quietly reproduce the center of gravity of the web. GSS·AI is the opposite by design: small, specialized, and built to hold counter-hegemonic perspectives as first-class, not as footnotes.

Three commitments

01

Specialized

Not a general-purpose assistant. Small, focused, and shaped around a deliberate corpus and a clear set of commitments.

02

Provenance-aware

Grounds its claims in a corpus of anti-imperialist journalism and decolonial scholarship, and cites where ideas come from.

03

Honest

Small and early by design. It won't pretend to certainty it doesn't have — a thinking partner to check, not an oracle.
