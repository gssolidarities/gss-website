// About the Project

DECOLONIZING THE ARCHIVE

Global South Solidarities is an open-access digital humanities project that centers voices, histories, and resistances from the Global South. Housed at Washington State University and led by Dr. Bibhushana Poudyal, GSS reimagines what an archive can be — not a static repository, but a living, breathing act of solidarity and remembrance.

Through interactive pedagogy, AI-assisted research, 3D artifact preservation, augmented reality reenactments, and multimedia storytelling, GSS builds bridges across geographies, temporalities, and disciplines.
