The U.S. National Archives and Records Administration has "signed agreements with social networks, publishing platforms, and content sharing sites with the goal of sharing our mission and connecting with more people than ever before." If we go to their "Social Media at the National Archives" page, we can see that they are using almost all of the above listed platforms to disseminate their materials, discourses, and rhetorics. So, why not we do the same? The point is not about using what the mainstream institutions are using. Far from it. The point is using whatever we can use to tell our stories ourselves. Let's see if master's tool can dismantle master's house.
