File CASE-01

ICE + AI Surveillance

From Slave Catchers to Flock Cameras — The Continuum of American State Violence.

AuthorAnastasia Garcia
FormatInteractive Sketchboard
Modules10 Scrollable Sections
Published14 May 2026
Surveillance Abolition Carceral Tech Flock Safety ICE Necropolitics
CLASSIFIED
Project Brief
A critical analysis of ICE + AI surveillance that traces the continuum from 1850s slave catchers to today's Flock cameras and automated deportation pipelines. The dossier reads as a classified field-report: scroll-driven hand-drawn timelines, FBI-style file badges, redacted-style "key insights," and follow-the-money diagrams connecting Flock Safety, Palantir, and private prison companies.
◈ AI Surveillance = High-Tech Slave Catching · Not a bug. Working as designed.

File CASE-02

AI & Modern Warfare

Narratives of US Military Force and Video Game Realities.

AuthorCecil Decker
FormatEssay / Zine (TBD)
SectionsCritique · Intervention · Archive
Published14 May 2026
Necropolitics Pentagon Anthropic / Claude Call of Duty Six Days in Fallujah Surveillance Capital
BRIEFING
Project Brief
A study of how AI contributes to narratives of combat based in simulation, with lethal consequences in the real world — and how propagandistic video games become entangled in those narratives. Centers Pete Hegseth's SpaceX speech on Grok's integration into Pentagon systems, the Anthropic / Claude controversy around its use in Iran, and the apoliticality claims of Call of Duty: Modern Warfare and Six Days in Fallujah.
◈ AI as both excuse and method for reducing military oversight.
